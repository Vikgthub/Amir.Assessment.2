Celeste Lapis

Built for the everyday driver who demands more, the Celeste Lapis pairs practical performance with a serene driving experience. Its sleek body hides a fuel-efficient yet responsive engine, making it perfect for urban travel and weekend getaways. The Lapis is the kind of sedan that doesn’t try to shout — it simply delivers, mile after mile.

  • Type: Sedan

  • Engine: 1.8L Inline-4

  • Top Speed: 200 km/h

  • 0-100 km/h: 8.3 seconds

  • Transmission: 6-speed CVT

  • Horsepower: 165 hp

  • Drive Type: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD)

  • Seats: 5

  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 50 liters

  • Price: Starts at €47,360
