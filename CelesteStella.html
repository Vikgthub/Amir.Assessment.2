Celeste Stella

Rediscover the joy of driving with the Celeste Stella — a cabriolet that invites you to escape the ordinary. Its retractable roof opens to the skies, offering a pure connection between you and the road. Elegant, sporty, and utterly liberating, Stella is summer in motion.

  • Top Speed: 250 km/h

  • 0-100 km/h: 6.7 seconds

  • Transmission: 6-speed dual-clutch

  • Horsepower: 365 hp

  • Drive Type: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

  • Seats: 2
